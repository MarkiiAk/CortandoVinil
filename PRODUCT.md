# PRODUCT.md

## Brand

**Name:** Cortando Vinil
**Domain:** cortandovinil.com
**Tagline:** Lo que diseñas, lo hacemos real.

## Register

**brand** — this is a marketing/landing site where the design IS the product. Visitors are deciding whether to trust and contact this company.

## Users

Primary: Business owners, entrepreneurs, designers, and event organizers in Mexico who need professional printing and vinyl-cutting services.

Secondary: Corporate clients needing merch (branded t-shirts, mugs, keychains).

Segments:
- Empresas (corporate): need bulk merch, branded material, professional quality
- Emprendedores (entrepreneurs): want small batches from 1 piece, product launches
- Eventos (events): lonas, roll ups, vinyl for events, signage

## Product Purpose

Cortando Vinil is a full-service production company. They don't sell finished consumer products — they produce custom items based on the client's files or specifications. Everything is quote-based via WhatsApp.

Services:
1. **Sublimado** — tazas, termos, mouse pads, fundas de celular, azulejos, llaveros, bolsas, rompecabezas
2. **Corte Láser y CNC** — wood, acrylic, metal, leather cutting/engraving; letreros, llaveros, portavasos, maquetas
3. **Gran Formato** — lonas, tela sublimada, vinil autoadherible, stands, roll ups, displays
4. **Impresos** — tarjetas de presentación, flyers, trípticos, pósters, papel autocopiante
5. **Vinil Textil** — playeras, gorras, sacos with detalle, flock/terciopelo, luminiscente, or printed vinyl

## Brand Voice

**Tone:** Direct, capable, confident. Not girly, not corporate-bland. Speaks to professionals who need things produced right.

Words: "producir", "cotizar", "supervisado", "exacto", "profesional", "a tu puerta"

Anti-references (what to avoid): Baby shower aesthetic, soft/pastel colors, serif italics, earth-tone Pinterest boards, "artesanal" vibes.

## Design System

**Strategy:** Committed color. Deep electric blue (tinta) dominates the hero — the color carries the brand voice.

**Palette:**
- `tinta` #1118C2 — electric blue (brand primary, hero)
- `fuego` #FF5A1A — vivid orange (CTAs, energy)
- `lienzo` #F7F7F5 — warm white (body bg)
- `oscuro` #0C0E1E — near-black (text)
- `pizarra` #545572 — slate (secondary text)

**Typography:**
- Headlines: Archivo Black — bold, geometric, no-nonsense
- Body: Figtree — clean, readable, professional

**Anti-patterns (absolute bans for this project):**
- Any serif or italic display fonts
- Soft/pastel color palette
- Baby shower or event/gift aesthetic
- "Artisanal" or handmade visual language
- Rounded, playful, or cute design elements
